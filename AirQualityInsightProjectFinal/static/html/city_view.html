About the Map

This map displays air quality index (AQI) data for cities across India. The data is color-coded according to the US EPA AQI scale:

  • Green (0-50): Good
  • Yellow (51-100): Moderate
  • Orange (101-150): Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups
  • Red (151-200): Unhealthy
  • Purple (201-300): Very Unhealthy
  • Maroon (301+): Hazardous

Click on any marker to see detailed information about that city's air quality.
